Details Panel


When you select an Actor, the Details Panel will display lots of information about that Actor, most of which is editable. At the top of the Details Panel is the name of the Actor, which can be changed.



To the right of that box you will see a Lock icon. By toggling the Lock icon to the locked setting, this will keep the details of the currently selected Actor locked in the Details Panel, even if other Actors are selected.

Property Matrix

To the right of the search box is a button that will launch the Property Matrix. The Property Matrix is for bulk comparison and editing of properties. Meaning it is used to compare and edit the values of properties of multiple Actors at once.
